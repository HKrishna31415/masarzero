# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Fuel station operators, terminal operators, engineering teams, regulators, investors, and commercial partners evaluating vapor recovery technology in real operating environments.

## Product Purpose

MasarZero presents vapor recovery systems, deployment proof, compliance positioning, and technical planning tools that help decision makers understand emissions capture, recovered product value, and operational fit.

## Brand Personality

Technical, credible, futuristic, and pragmatic. The interface should feel advanced without making engineering claims feel decorative or unserious.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboard fluff, toy-like estimators, vague financial-first ROI framing on engineering calculators, and ornamental UI that distracts from measured vapor recovery logic.

## Design Principles

- Lead with operational proof and transparent assumptions.
- Make engineering math inspectable enough for serious buyers.
- Keep commercial upside separate from emissions and recovery calculations when the user is doing technical evaluation.
- Preserve a high-confidence industrial tone across public pages and tool surfaces.
- Prefer clear controls and readable outputs over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-usable controls, readable labels, and reduced-motion-safe interactions.
